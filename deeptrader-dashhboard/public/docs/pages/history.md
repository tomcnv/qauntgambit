# Trade History

Browse and analyze all completed trades with advanced filtering and PnL breakdown.

## Widgets & Cards

### Date Range Calendar
Date picker for selecting the analysis window. Presets: Today, 1W, 1M, 3M, YTD, All.

### Filters Bar
Symbol, side, PnL direction, min/max size, exchange filters.

### Trade Table
Paginated table: Time, Symbol, Side, Entry, Exit, Size, Gross PnL, Fees, Net PnL, Hold Time, Exit Reason.

## Modals & Drawers

### Trade Inspector
Full trade lifecycle: signal context, decision trace, order fills, PnL attribution, slippage analysis.

## Actions

- Filter trades by any column
- Sort by PnL, time, symbol
- Export filtered trades to CSV
- Open trade inspector for any row
- Compare two trades side-by-side
