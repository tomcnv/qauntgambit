# Market Context

Analyze current market conditions including regime detection, volatility regimes, and symbol-level context.

## Widgets & Cards

### Filters Bar
Filter by symbol, timeframe, and regime type.

### Regime Indicators
Current market regime classification: trending, mean-reverting, volatile, quiet.

### Profile Score
Strategy profile fitness score for current market conditions.

### Profile Rejection
Reasons why certain profiles are being rejected in current conditions.

## Modals & Drawers

### Symbol Drawer
Detailed market context for a single symbol: regime history, volatility, correlation, orderbook depth.

## Actions

- Filter by symbol or regime type
- View regime transition history
- Compare current vs historical regimes
- Export market context data
