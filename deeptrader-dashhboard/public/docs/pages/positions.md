# Positions

View and manage all open positions across the fleet with real-time exposure and risk metrics.

## Widgets & Cards

### Risk Summary Strip
Total Exposure, Margin Used, Available Margin, Liquidation Distance, Position Count.

### Positions Table
Live-updating table: Symbol, Side, Size, Entry Price, Mark Price, Unrealized PnL, PnL %, Exposure USD.

### Exposure Breakdown
Pie/treemap chart showing exposure by symbol, with drill-down to individual positions.

### Margin & Liquidation
Gauge showing margin utilization and distance to liquidation price per position.

## Modals & Drawers

### Position Detail Drawer
Full position view: entry time, average price, partial fills, funding payments, liquidation price, hedge status.

## Actions

- Close a position (market or limit)
- Reduce position size
- Set take-profit / stop-loss
- Filter by symbol, side, PnL direction
- Sort by any column

## Settings & Knobs

- Mark price source (last trade vs index)
- PnL display currency (USD, BTC, %)
- Position grouping (by symbol, by bot, flat)
