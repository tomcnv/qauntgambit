# Exposure

Analyze portfolio exposure over time with breakdowns by symbol, side, and asset class.

## Widgets & Cards

### Date Range Calendar
Select analysis period for historical exposure charts.

### Exposure Over Time
Area chart showing total long/short exposure over the selected period.

### Exposure by Symbol
Treemap or bar chart of current exposure allocation across symbols.

### Historical Heatmap
Calendar heatmap showing daily peak exposure levels.

## Actions

- Select date range for analysis
- Toggle between USD and percentage view
- Export exposure data
- Drill down into symbol-level exposure
