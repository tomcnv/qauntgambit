# Incidents

Track and review all risk incidents: limit breaches, circuit breaker triggers, and anomalous events.

## Widgets & Cards

### Incident List
Chronological list of incidents with severity badges (critical, warning, info), timestamps, and affected symbols.

### Severity Indicators
Color-coded severity: red (critical), amber (warning), blue (info). Count badges per severity level.

## Modals & Drawers

### Post-Mortem Detail
Expandable incident detail: root cause, timeline of events, positions affected, P&L impact, resolution steps taken.

## Actions

- Filter incidents by severity, date range, symbol
- Mark incident as reviewed
- Add post-mortem notes
- Export incident report
